05

Elevation

Elevation is told through light arriving from above (a 1px rim-light), not shadow pooling below. Each step lightens one level — never a jump straight to gray.

The signature mark

One recurring device, reused everywhere it applies, rather than a different affordance invented per screen.

The Gnomon — named for the angled shard on a sundial that casts the precise line of light. Wherever it appears, it means the same thing: here is the exact point.
